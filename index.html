Questions That Clients Ask

How much does it cost to add a room?

This depends on the size of the room and the quality of finishes selected. The current industry average cost to build is R 15,730 per square metre, but this is a ballpark figure. We work within your budget to find the best solution.

Can I build up?

We would need to assess your home's foundation and load-bearing capacity. Adding another floor is an effective use of space and can completely transform your home.

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

A full kitchen refurbishment typically takes between two weeks to two months, depending on the project's complexity and your preferences.

What does a new kitchen cost?

A modest kitchen remodel starts around R 100,000, while high-end renovations typically exceed R150,000. Contact us for a detailed, obligation-free quote.

Do I need an architect to renovate?

While many projects can be handled by a contractor, complex renovations may require an architect or engineer. We'll guide you through the process.
